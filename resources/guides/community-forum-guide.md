The AlgoTradingWithIghodalo Community Forum (hosted on Telegram) is the official hub for traders to connect, ask questions, share insights, and receive announcements about EA improvements.

## Purpose

- Peer-to-peer support from experienced users  
- Share optimization ideas and best practices  
- Discuss live market conditions and EA behavior  
- Learn how to deploy EAs effectively  
- Receive official updates and maintenance notices

## What you can do

- Ask technical questions (installation, VPS, configuration)  
- Share performance insights and broker feedback  
- Access user-generated resources and tips  
- Receive EA update alerts and maintenance schedules

## Community rules

- Stay respectful and constructive  
- No spam, harassment, or irrelevant content  
- Do not share private or sensitive information  
- No redistribution of paid EAs or proprietary files

## Support provided

Community admins and support staff help by:

- Answering setup questions  
- Sharing recommended settings  
- Clarifying EA functionality  
- Announcing updates, maintenance windows, and live sessions

## How to join

- The community is hosted on **Telegram**  
- **Official Channel:** announcements and updates  
- **Community Chat:** live discussions and support  
- Join links are available on the official website and purchase confirmation emails

## Benefits

- Faster answers to your questions  
- Shared knowledge from experienced traders  
- Real-time EA update alerts  
- Access to special events and community sessions  
- Learn collaboratively with other AlgoTradingWithIghodalo users
